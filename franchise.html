Franchise Basha

Canada's growing halal Mediterranean franchise. The Kelowna location is family-run; new locations across Canada open through the Basha Chef Group.

Why Basha franchises work

Donair and shawarma have outpaced burgers as the fastest-growing Canadian quick-service category over the last decade. Halal certification is a moat — only ~10% of Canadian restaurants are certified, but Muslim Canadians are the fastest-growing demographic in major metros. Basha sits at the intersection: authentic recipes, certified supply chain, and a price point ($14-20 per entrée) that competes with burger chains.

What's included

Recipe library (donair spice blend, garlic sauce, marinades, sauces). Brand guidelines + assets. Operational playbook (kitchen workflow, supplier contacts, certification paperwork). Marketing kit (templated press releases, social, GBP setup). Equipment specs (vertical spits, fryers, prep stations). Site selection support.
