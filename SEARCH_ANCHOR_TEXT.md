# Search Anchor Text — When You Pitch for Backlinks

> **What this is:** When journalists, bloggers, or directories link to your site, the words they use as the link itself is "anchor text." Google reads anchor text as a topical signal. Manage it.

## The 3 anchor categories

### 1. Branded anchors (50% of links)

Use these for: Castanet, Kelowna Now, Tourism Kelowna, friend recommendations, generic listings.

```
- Basha Donair Kelowna
- Basha Donair & Shawarma
- Basha Donair Rutland
- Basha (Kelowna)
- bashachef.com (when linking to franchise parent)
```

### 2. Topical / branded-hybrid (30% of links)

Use these for: food blogs, tourism sites, food-comparison articles.

```
- Basha Donair — Kelowna's halal Mediterranean kitchen
- Basha Donair & Shawarma in Rutland
- Basha Donair, the family-run halal spot in Kelowna
- Basha — a Rutland halal restaurant
```

### 3. Naked URL / generic (20% of links)

Use these to look organic and avoid over-optimization penalties.

```
- https://website-ffb90ac7.lfg.obp.mybluehost.me
- (link)
- here
- this restaurant
```

---

## ❌ What you NEVER want as anchor text

If a backlink uses these as the link words, **politely ask for it to be changed**. Over-optimization on these triggers Google's spam filter:

```
- best donair Kelowna
- top halal restaurant Kelowna
- #1 donair shop
- cheap halal Kelowna
- Kelowna's best donair
```

These look like SEO spam. They hurt more than help when over-used.

## Specific link requests by source

When pitching each outreach target, suggest the EXACT anchor text you want them to use. Make it easy for them.

### Castanet feature article

> "Could you link to us as **'Basha Donair Kelowna'** when you mention the restaurant? Quick clickable text for readers."

### Kelowna Now restaurant guide

> "If you list us in the guide, **'Basha Donair & Shawarma'** would be perfect — that's our full business name."

### Tourism Kelowna directory listing

> "Listing as **'Basha Donair Kelowna — halal Mediterranean'** matches how customers search for us."

### Uptown Rutland BIA member directory

> "**'Basha Donair & Shawarma · Rutland'** — that captures our neighbourhood + brand."

### UBCO student newspaper / ISA

> "Linking as **'Basha Donair'** with the campus distance ('5 min from UBCO') as separate copy works well."

## Where to scatter URLs (to look natural)

When a journalist or blog covers you, suggest 2 internal links:

1. **Homepage** as the brand anchor
2. **Topic-relevant deep page** as the topical anchor

| Their topic | Deep page to suggest |
|---|---|
| "Best Kelowna halal" article | `/halal-kelowna.html` |
| "Best donair" comparison | `/best-donair-kelowna.html` |
| "Rutland food guide" | `/donair-rutland.html` |
| "UBCO food roundup" | `/halal-near-ubco.html` |
| "Catering for offices" | `/catering.html` |
| Recipe / food history | `/journal/donair-vs-shawarma-vs-gyro.html` |
| Late night food | `/late-night-food-kelowna.html` |
| "Cheap eats" piece | `/student-discount.html` |

This pattern (1 brand + 1 deep page) is exactly what Google likes to see for natural-looking link profiles.

## Anchor text ratio (90-day target)

After 90 days of outreach:
- **45-55% branded** (Basha Donair Kelowna, Basha Donair, Basha)
- **25-35% topical hybrid** (Basha Donair, halal Kelowna)
- **15-25% naked URL / generic** (link, here, the URL itself)
- **0% commercial** (best donair, top halal — none)

## Tools to verify your link profile

**Free**:
- Google Search Console → Links → "Top linking sites" + "Top linking text"
- Bing Webmaster Tools → Backlinks
- Ahrefs free trial (7 days, monthly) — see exact anchor text breakdown

**Paid (worth it after first 50 backlinks)**:
- Ahrefs ($99/mo) — most accurate
- SEMrush ($99/mo) — strong competitor analysis (compare to Tahini's link profile)
- Moz Pro ($99/mo) — easier UI

For now: Google Search Console is enough.

---

## Detecting anchor-text problems early

Red flag patterns that mean Google might penalize:
- Same exact-match anchor text on 3+ different domains in same month
- More than 30% of backlinks use commercial anchors ("best donair", etc.)
- Sudden burst of 20+ backlinks in 1 week from low-quality directories

If you see any of these in Search Console: stop new outreach for 2 weeks, do a bunch of branded/generic link-building to dilute the ratio.

## Boilerplate "request a backlink update" email

When a backlink uses bad anchor text:

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks again for including Basha Donair in [article title]. Quick favour:
could you update the anchor text from "best halal Kelowna" to just
"Basha Donair Kelowna"? We're trying to keep our backlink profile
natural for SEO, and exact-match commercial anchors flag against us.

Either of these works:
- Basha Donair Kelowna
- Basha Donair & Shawarma

Thanks!
[Your name]
Basha Donair · 101-135 Rutland Rd N
```

90% of the time this gets honored within a week.
