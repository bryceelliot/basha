What's in a Donair Poutine?

Four things, layered. (1) Crispy fries — thick-cut, fried fresh to order. (2) Cheese curds — real, fresh, the kind that squeak. (3) Brown gravy — house-made, rich, salty enough to balance the meat. (4) Slow-roasted donair — spiced ground beef carved off the vertical spit and warmed through. The fries stay crispy underneath. The curds melt slightly. The gravy ties everything. The donair adds protein and depth. It's a meal.
