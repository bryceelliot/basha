The Mediterranean menu in Rutland

Mediterranean cuisine in Rutland means more than just one dish — it's a whole table. At Basha we serve the full Levantine spread: hummus and tahini drizzled with olive oil and za'atar, tabbouleh dense with parsley, fattoush bright with sumac, vertical-spit donair and shawarma, falafel fried to order, grilled meats and fresh-baked pita. Every dish is halal-certified. Every recipe is family-passed.

Beyond the wrap

Most Rutland customers find Basha because of donair or shawarma — but stay for the mezze. Our hummus is made fresh every morning from chickpeas we cook in-house. Our tabbouleh has 4× the parsley of most North American versions because that's how it should be. Our garlic sauce is whipped daily. If you've only had wraps from us, come in for a mezze plate next time.
