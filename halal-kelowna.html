The Short Answer

Where to Find Halal Food in Kelowna

Finding halal food in Kelowna can be a challenge — the city's dining scene is expanding, but certified halal options remain limited. Basha Donair & Shawarma has been Kelowna's go-to answer since the franchise arrived in the Okanagan.

Located at 101-135 Rutland Rd N in the heart of Kelowna's Rutland neighbourhood, we serve a full menu of authentic Mediterranean dishes that are 100% halal certified — every protein, every ingredient, every time. No exceptions, no mixed kitchens.

Whether you're a longtime Kelowna resident, a student at UBCO, or visiting the Okanagan, Basha is the restaurant where the whole group can eat without question.

What Makes Basha Different

In a city where Mediterranean and Middle Eastern restaurants are rare, Basha Kelowna stands out for three reasons:

  • 1.
    Fully certified halal — not just halal-friendly.

    Many restaurants claim halal-friendly options but use shared kitchens or source proteins that aren't certified. At Basha, the entire menu is halal. There's no guesswork.

  • 2.
    Made fresh daily, never frozen.

    The spit rotates fresh every day. Sauces are house-made. Vegetables are prepped fresh each morning. Quality is built into the process, not just the marketing.

  • 3.
    Part of a trusted Canadian franchise.

    Basha is part of the Basha Chef franchise, one of Canada's established Mediterranean chains. Decades of recipe development and quality control behind every dish.
