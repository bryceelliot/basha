# Customer Review Request Templates

> **Goal:** Get to 200+ Google reviews on Basha Donair Kelowna in 90 days. Currently at ~487 (placeholder schema). Real review count drives "near me" rankings more than any other on-page work.

## When to ask

- **Best moment**: customer is paying / handing back the receipt and clearly enjoyed the meal
- **Second best**: 2 hours after a delivery order (text via SkipTheDishes/DoorDash isn't possible — use receipt printout instead)
- **Avoid**: rushed lunch crowd, anyone who looks unhappy
- **Frequency goal**: 5–10 review asks per day → ~3% conversion → 1–3 new reviews per day → **30–90 reviews/month**

---

## Template 1 — Verbal ask at the counter

> "Hey, glad you enjoyed it. Real quick — would you mind dropping us a Google review? Family-run spot, every review really helps us get found. There's a QR code right here on the receipt." *[gesture to QR card on counter]*

**Why it works:** Specific, asks for the action immediately, gives a tangible next step. "Family-run" creates emotional buy-in.

## What NOT to do

- ❌ **Don't ask for "5 star" reviews specifically** — Google's policy violation; can get reviews removed
- ❌ **Don't offer a discount in exchange for a review** — illegal in some jurisdictions, against TOS in all
- ❌ **Don't post fake reviews** — Google detects this and will ban your GBP
- ❌ **Don't ask the same customer twice** — wait at least 6 months before re-asking
- ❌ **Don't ignore negative reviews** — respond within 24 hours, professionally, take it offline if you can

## What TO do

- ✅ **Respond to every review** within 24 hours — Google rewards engaged owners
- ✅ **Respond differently to each review** — don't copy-paste, personalize
- ✅ **Mention specific menu items** customers reference in their review (signals semantic relevance)
- ✅ **Use neighbourhood + city keywords** in your responses ("glad we're your Rutland go-to" / "Kelowna deserves real halal")
- ✅ **Follow up with friends/regulars** — they're your best chance for fast review velocity in week 1
