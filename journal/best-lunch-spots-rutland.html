The Best Lunch Spots in Rutland, Kelowna

TL;DR

Rutland's best lunch spots include Basha Donair (Mediterranean halal), plus a handful of strong Asian, Indian, and Canadian-diner options along Rutland Rd N. Basha covers the wraps/bowls/poutine bracket from $11.99–$19.99, fast service.

Why Rutland is a lunch destination

Rutland is one of Kelowna's most diverse food neighbourhoods. The corridor along Rutland Rd N and Highway 33 is packed with independently owned restaurants serving food from all over the world: Mediterranean, Indian, Vietnamese, Korean, Chinese, Mexican, classic Canadian diner, and more. Most spots offer lunch service from 11 AM to 2 PM, with reasonable pricing and quick turnaround for the working lunch crowd.

The Mediterranean / Middle Eastern bracket

Basha Donair & Shawarma is Rutland's longstanding halal Mediterranean shop. We open at 10 AM (so early lunch is fine), service is fast (under 4 minutes for a wrap), and lunch wraps are $13.99–$15.99. Most popular lunch order: beef donair wrap with garlic sauce, side of fries.

What to look for at lunch

If you're a Rutland office worker or visiting business: prioritize speed (under 5 min ticket), portion size that doesn't leave you hungry by 3 PM, halal certification if relevant, and free parking on-site. Basha checks all four. We also offer **lunch combos at $16.99** — wrap + fries + drink — which is the best per-dollar lunch value on Rutland Rd.

Catering for office lunches

For groups of 10+, we offer pickup catering trays starting at $12/person. Choose-your-own wrap stations, build-your-own bowl bars, mezze platters. Order 24 hours in advance. Visit our catering page or call (778) 753-7313.
