The Basha Legacy

Thirty Years of
Mediterranean Mastery

The Basha story began with Chef Kass Fattah — a culinary visionary with over three decades of experience in the food service industry. His passion? Bringing the vibrant, bold flavours of the Mediterranean to Canada, made accessible and affordable for everyone.

From his very first location, Chef Fattah built a reputation on three non-negotiable principles: fresh ingredients, bold flavours, and unwavering halal standards. No frozen shortcuts. No compromises on quality. Just food that's made with heart and served with pride.

Today, Basha has grown into a beloved Canadian franchise — and we're honoured to bring that same tradition to Kelowna's Rutland neighbourhood, where we've become a local staple for authentic Mediterranean eats.

Know Your Food

Donair vs. Shawarma

Both are delicious — but do you know the difference?

The Donair

The donair is a uniquely Canadian creation — a western take on the traditional Middle Eastern kebab. At Basha, our donair is made with seasoned ground beef formed on a large skewer, slow-cooked on a rotating spit, then sliced fresh to order.

The result is juicy, tender meat packed with bold spice. Wrapped in warm pita with our signature sweet garlic sauce, fresh tomatoes and onions — it's something you have to taste to believe.

The Shawarma

Shawarma is the original — a Middle Eastern staple dating back centuries. Unlike the donair, shawarma is made from marinated sliced meat (chicken, beef, or lamb) stacked in layers on a rotating vertical spit and slow-roasted to perfection.

The marinade is everything — our signature blend of cumin, turmeric, coriander, cardamom and more creates a depth of flavour that's unmistakably Mediterranean. Served in a wrap or over rice, it's an authentic taste of the old world.
