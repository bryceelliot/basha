Why Rutland orders poutine here

Most Rutland spots that serve poutine use shredded cheese, frozen fries, or jarred gravy. We don't. Real cheese curds (the squeaky kind), thick-cut fries fried fresh to order, and house-made brown gravy. Add the spit-roasted donair meat and you have what Rutland calls the perfect 9 PM dinner.
