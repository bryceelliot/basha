Cross-contact information

Basha operates a halal kitchen with shared cooking surfaces. While our vegetarian items contain no meat, they share fryer oil with our falafel (vegetarian) — the falafel itself never shares oil with meat. If you have strict cross-contact requirements, please let us know when you order and we'll work with you.
