About Basha Kelowna

The Taste of the
Mediterranean,
Right Here in Kelowna

Basha Donair & Shawarma brings over three decades of authentic Mediterranean culinary tradition to Kelowna's Rutland neighbourhood. We're the Okanagan's go-to destination for genuine halal donairs, shawarma, falafel, and so much more.

Chef Kass Fattah's legacy lives in every wrap, bowl, and plate we serve. Made with fresh, quality ingredients — no shortcuts, no compromises. Just bold flavours the way they were meant to be made.
