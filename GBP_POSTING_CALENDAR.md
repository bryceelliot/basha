# Google Business Profile — 12-Week Posting Calendar

> **Goal:** Post 2–3 times per week to GBP. Posts are a top-3 ranking signal for "near me" searches and are wildly underused by competitors. Tahini's Kelowna posts ~once/month — outpost them and you outrank them.

## How to post

1. Sign in to https://business.google.com
2. Find your Basha Donair listing → click "Posts" or "Add update"
3. Pick post type (Update / Offer / Event / What's New)
4. Add photo (REQUIRED — text-only posts get less reach)
5. Write copy (max 1,500 chars, but keep under 250 for engagement)
6. Add CTA button (Order online / Call / Learn more)
7. Publish

**Post types and their lifespans:**
- **Update**: 7 days (use for menu items, daily features)
- **Offer**: customizable date range (use for coupons, specials)
- **Event**: dates required (use for new menu launches, holiday hours)

## Frequency rules

- **Minimum**: 2 posts/week
- **Optimal**: 3 posts/week (Mon/Wed/Fri OR Tue/Thu/Sat)
- **Maximum**: 1 post/day (more than this and engagement drops)
- **Best times**: 11 AM (lunch crowd) or 5 PM (dinner crowd) Pacific time

## Photo guidelines

- 1200×900 minimum (Google compresses, but starts from your upload)
- Bright, food-forward, no text overlay
- Reuse from `images/food/` and `images/menu/` — every photo on the site is fair game

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't post the same photo twice in a month
- ❌ Don't write longer than 250 characters (engagement craters)
- ❌ Don't use stock photos (Google penalizes)
- ❌ Don't post text-only updates (low reach)
- ❌ Don't fight with negative reviewers in posts (respond in the review thread instead)

## Performance tracking

Each post shows views + clicks in your GBP dashboard. After 30 days:
- **Views per post**: aim for 200+
- **Click-through rate**: aim for 2-4%
- **Calls/direction-clicks generated**: aim for 5+/post

If a post is hitting 500+ views and 5%+ CTR, **double down on that style** for the next month.
