Story (boilerplate)

Short version: Basha Donair & Shawarma is Kelowna's family-run halal Mediterranean restaurant in Rutland, part of the Basha Chef Group — Canada's growing halal donair franchise. Slow-roasted spit donair, hand-marinated shawarma, house-made falafel, full mezze. 100% halal certified.

Long version: The donair as Canadians know it was invented in Halifax in 1973 by a Greek-Canadian named Peter Gamoulakos who couldn't sell traditional lamb gyro to Maritimes customers. He swapped to spiced ground beef and a sweet condensed-milk sauce. The dish spread west through the 80s and 90s. Western Canada developed its own variation — savoury garlic sauce, deeper spice profile. Basha Donair brought a Lebanese-tradition take on that Western Canadian donair to Kelowna, with strict halal certification and family recipes passed down for generations.
